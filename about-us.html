About Settle N Pay, Inc


Comprehensive National Debt Collection Services
Established in 2009, Settle N Pay Inc., has become a national leader in debt recovery with vast experience providing collection services to major utility companies and healthcare providers nationwide. We are a nationally licensed debt collection .

Settle N Pay, Inc. offers comprehensive debt collection services nationwide including first party, early-out collection & billing services. Our strengths are based on innovative, resourceful and aggressive debt collection practices that are continuously developed using the latest technology. From high-capacity, networked call centers in all US time zones to the most highly trained debt collectors, maximizing the recovery of debt is our primary mission.

Our list of clients throughout the country is extensive and available upon request. Our client lists includes businesses from all industries:
retail • medical • real estate • entertainment • utilities • banking • check collections • commercial
