The Settle N Pay, Inc.

The Settle N Pay, Inc. maintains strict compliance with all applicable State and Federal laws including, but not limited to:

The Dodd-Frank Act
Graham Leach Bliley Act (GLBA)
Fair Debt Collections Practices Act (FDCPA)
Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA)
Fair Credit Transaction Act (FACTA)
Telephone Consumers Protection Act (TCPA).

Consumers contacting The Settle N Pay, Inc. have the choice to engage our automated system or speak with a live customer representative. All outbound telephone calls are manually dialed by live human agents until express consent is obtained or is withdrawn.

With our proprietary reliable high speed phone communication system call system, all telephone campaigns are executed and managed in-house by The Settle N Pay, Inc. and all outbound calls are initiated and transfered between highly trained The Settle N Paycall center representatives. Our reliable high speed phone communication system platform is entirely seperate and independent of auto-dialing systems, ensuring complete FDCPA & TCPA regulatory compliance.

This human intiated telephone dialing system allows for full legal compliance with all Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) & Fair Debt Collections Practices Act (FDCPA) requirements and guidelines, as well as maintaining compliance in accordance with the latest court rulings and precedents affecting the application of the TCPA.
